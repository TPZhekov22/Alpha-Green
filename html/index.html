Welcome to Alpha Green, where nature and innovation converge to create a sustainable future. Explore our eco-friendly products and join us in the journey towards a greener planet.

  1. Soil Pollution:
    • Sources: Pesticides, industrial waste, improper disposal.
    • Effects: Soil degradation, loss of biodiversity, crop contamination.
    • Solutions: Sustainable agriculture, limit chemical use, remediate contaminated sites.
  2. Noise Pollution:
    • Sources: Traffic, industrial machinery, construction.
    • Effects: Hearing loss, stress, sleep disturbances.
    • Solutions: Noise barriers, regulate urban noise, raise awareness.
  3. Light Pollution:
    • Sources: Excessive urban lighting.
    • Effects: Ecosystem disruption, health issues, astronomy interference.
    • Solutions: Energy-efficient lighting, motion-activated lights, responsible lighting.
  4. Plastic Pollution:
    • Sources: Improper plastic disposal, microplastics.
    • Effects: Harm to marine life, ecosystem disruption.
    • Solutions: Reduce plastic use, promote recycling, support cleanup initiatives.
